How It Works

Our comprehensive design process ensures every project receives the attention and expertise it deserves, from initial consultation to final installation.

1

Design Consultation

Every project begins with understanding your goals, your property, and your budget.

2

Conceptualization

If we decide to take on your project, we'll conceptualize the overall creative direction to lay the foundation for a successful installation.

3

Procurement

Next, we'll procure the furniture, textiles, accessories that will go into your property. Depending on project parameters, conceptualization can happen iteratively alongside procurement.

4

Installation

Installation is where everything comes together. We use principles like balance, proportion, and layering to make sure your property shines.
